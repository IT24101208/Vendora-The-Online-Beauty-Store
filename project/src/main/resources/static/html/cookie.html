What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) by websites you visit. They act like a short-term memory, allowing the site to recognize your device and remember information about your visit.

We use cookies to make your browsing experience better and more personalized.

Types of Cookies:
  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored on your device for longer periods to remember your preferences.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality, such as secure login sessions or shopping cart memory.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site to improve performance and content.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalization, like language or region preferences.
  • Advertising & Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads based on your interests.
  • Social Media Cookies: Enable social sharing features directly from our site.

Third-Party Cookies

We may partner with trusted third parties who place cookies on your device to support analytics or advertising services. These cookies are controlled by the third party, and we do not have direct control over them.

Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You can choose how cookies are managed on your device:

  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Use browser tools or extensions to manage cookie preferences.

Note: Blocking or disabling cookies may affect website functionality or prevent certain features from working.
