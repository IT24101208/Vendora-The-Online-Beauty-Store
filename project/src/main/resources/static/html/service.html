Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need an account to place an order?
Yes, you need to create an account to place orders and track your purchases.

2. How do I place an order?
Browse products, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.

3. What payment methods are available?
We accept Credit/Debit Cards and Cash on Delivery (COD) for your convenience.

4. How can I track my order?
You can track your order through the "Track Orders" section in your account after logging in.

5. Can I cancel or modify my order?
Yes, you can cancel or modify your order before it has been shipped.

6. How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes between 3–5 working days depending on your location.

7. What should I do if I receive a damaged or wrong product?
Please contact our support team immediately to request a replacement or refund.

8. Are the products authentic?
Yes, all products sold on Vendora are 100% genuine and sourced from trusted suppliers.

9. Can I return a product?
Yes, returns are accepted within a limited time period if the product meets our return policy conditions.

10. How can I contact customer support?
You can contact us via the contact form or email at the bottom of this page
